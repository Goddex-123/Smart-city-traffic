# Executive Summary: Smart City Traffic System

## Project Overview

The Smart City Traffic Congestion Prediction & Optimization System is an AI-powered solution designed to reduce urban traffic congestion through predictive analytics and intelligent signal optimization.

## Business Problem

Urban traffic congestion costs the global economy over $500 billion annually in lost productivity, fuel waste, and environmental damage. Traditional traffic management systems are:
- **Reactive** rather than proactive
- **Fixed-timing** signals that don't adapt to traffic patterns  
- **Inefficient** in handling peak-hour congestion
- **Limited visibility** into future traffic conditions

## Solution

Our system provides a comprehensive, data-driven approach:

1. **Real-time Traffic Monitoring** across 120+ road segments
2. **AI-Powered Prediction** of congestion 1-2 hours ahead
3. **Intelligent Signal Optimization** to minimize wait times
4. **Environmental Impact** tracking and reduction
5. **Economic Benefits** quantification
